CHECKPOINT 2

The Canopy

Standing 73 feet high, this beautiful cupola rests directly behind the main monument. It was constructed as a stunning homage to the architecture of Mahabalipuram pavilion.

Built In
1936
Height
73 Feet
Architect
E. Lutyens

About the Structure

The Canopy originally housed a stunning 50-foot marble statue of King George V. Following India's independence, the statue was relocated to Coronation Park in 1968. Today, the elegant empty dome stands as a symbol of the nation's sovereign history, framed beautifully through the arch of India Gate.
